ABOUT US

Advanced real-time churn prevention

The moment any device attempts to connect to your site or app, you’ll be able to spot user behaviors and device information that leads to fraud, and stop that fraud attempt in real time.our community of analysts add evidence to make this fraud detection and prevention intelligence even more powerful and effective.

Our Mission

We are looking to design, develop and provide a comprehensive fraud detection modeling system able to undertand cusomer behaviour and automatically detect in real-time and adaptive manner the frauds through analyzing the past transaction details of the customers and extract the behavioral patterns to set the model. To reduce losses due to payment fraud for both merchants and electronic payment company. ||||||| We intend to reach every inch of Palestine with our solution, to be the leading FinTech solution in fraud detection, promote and empower merchants and financial institutions to adopt our solution, raise the awareness about the importance our solution. We commit to operate in an economically, socially and environmentally responsible manner. To establish lasting partnerships through the delivery of innovative, reliable, and secure payments solutions, focused on enriching customer experience.

Our Vision

Tele-Seer vision is to be the most trusted and innovative fraud detection, prevention and management solution in the digital transaction industry, delivering best-in-class solutions to our valued customers in the markets we serve and secure the flow of digital transaction.

Our Team

Tele-Seer has the right team, which consists of a diverse range of education backgrounds with the right mix of work experience in the field of Tele-Seer startup. It has a data scientist for analyzing and translating the data using sophisticated technology programs; a software engineer to evaluate and recommend tools, technologies and processes to ensure the highest quality of provided services; a data engineer to find trends in data sets and develop new risk factors for fraudulent actions to optimize the software; a programmer to write the optimal code, maintain standards and optimization, address the issues of testing and quality, and move the correct code to production; business development to identify new business opportunities for Tele-Seer including new markets, growth areas, trends, customers, and services; a business analyst to create a detailed business analysis, outlining problems, opportunities and solutions for a Tele-Seer.
